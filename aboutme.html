Pain Killer

She is virtually indestructible. With her exceptional regenerative abilities, minor injuries heal in seconds,
and do not even slow her down; more major ones tend to take a few minutes.
explosions, chemical weapons assault, axes buried in her spine, even a shotgun blast to the face.

Mission

Rasinga Lee begins as an undercover police officer attempting to infiltrate the Mob. After gaining the
trust of mob boss, she is given the assignment of passing a message on to rival gang member Saske, not
realizing that an explosive device has been planted on her. As she meets her target, the explosion causes
significant injuries, but Saske is uninjured. Through mysterious means, he manages to revive Lee, giving her
superhuman regenerative powers in the process. Leaving her life as a police officer behind, she becomes the
vigilante Painkiller Lee.Her mission is to clear all the gangsters in the city.
